ALFREDO PASTA

SO CREAMY and simple to make!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1.5 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Teaspoons Garlic Minced
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Pepper
  • 2 Cups Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese (or any other cheese you have)

Instructions

  1. Boil a box of pasta of your choosing.
  2. Add the butter and cream to a large skillet.
  3. Simmer over low heat for 2 minutes.
  4. Whisk in the garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper for one minute.
  5. Whisk in the parmesan cheese until melted.
  6. Serve immediately.
